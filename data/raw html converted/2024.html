The 'Trends' section allows you to see which companies are the biggest movers across many categories, each on a separate tab:

  • Popular, which lists the stocks with the highest gains in Google query traffic
  • Price, which lists the top gainers and losers in terms of % price change vs. the prior day's official closing price
  • Mkt Cap (market cap), which identifies the top gainers and losers in terms of change in absolute market capitalization vs. the prior day's official closing market cap
  • Volume, which lists the stocks with the highest trade volume

Note: Price, Market cap, and Volume exclude stocks with market caps less than $1 billion and stock prices lower than $1.
