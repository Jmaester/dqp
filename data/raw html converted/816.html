Our Team

Aside from the community, there’s a hard-working team behind the Digg site. Founded by Kevin Rose and launched in November 2004, Digg has grown to be one of the most popular sources of information on the Web through the efforts of a passionate group of developers, database administrators, designers, and other individuals. The team here at Digg contribute and use a wide variety of open source projects. If you’re interested in joining us, we’re looking for more talented people to join Digg, so be sure to check out our jobs page.
