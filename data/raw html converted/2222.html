Key Features

Google Desktop for Enterprise helps users gain control over the ever-growing mountain of information located on their computers and includes administrator features that provide enhanced security, centralized configuration and easy deployment.

Benefits for users

Google search for the computer

With Google Desktop for Enterprise, users can search the full text of their email, files, viewed web pages and chats. Specifically, they can search:

  • Email from Gmail, IBM Lotus Notes, Outlook 2000+, Outlook Express 5+, Netscape Mail 7.1+, Mozilla Mail 1.4+ and Thunderbird
  • All the most popular business file types on their Windows-based PC, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, PDF, MP3, ZIP files, image, audio and video files
  • Web pages you've viewed using Internet Explorer 5+, Netscape 7.1+, Mozilla 1.4+ and Firefox
  • Chats from Google Talk, AOL Instant Messenger 5+, and MSN Messenger

A single place to search

When used in conjunction with the Google Search Appliance or Google Mini, our enterprise search appliances, Google Desktop for Enterprise gives employees a centralized way to search their PCs, the corporate network and the Internet. The results are returned in an easy-to-navigate interface that users are already familiar with.

More information at their fingertips

In addition to its basic search functionality, Google Desktop for Enterprise introduces new ways to access relevant and time sensitive information.
