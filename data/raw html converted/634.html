What is Google Checkout?

Google Checkout allows you to shop faster on the web, and from your mobile device.

We protect your credit card information and email address so you can always shop with confidence.

When you are ready to purchase an item from your mobile device, just click the Google Checkout button and sign in with your login information, or follow the prompts to create a new account.

For information about the Google Checkout terms of use and the general terms of use for Google services, please visit our Terms of Service.

How do I create a Google Checkout account from my mobile device?

If you've signed up for Google Checkout from a desktop computer, you don't need to create a new account to make purchases from your mobile device.

Simply enter your username and password when prompted to complete a purchase.

If you haven't used Google Checkout but you'd like to buy digital content from your mobile phone, just follow these steps to start shopping:

  1. Locate the item you'd like to buy, and place it in your shopping cart.
  2. Select Google Checkout as your payment method.
  3. Click Sign up now to complete your purchase on the Google Checkout order page.
  4. Enter the required information, and click Continue.
  5. Select a Checkout PIN, and click Continue.
  6. On the Items page, enter your Checkout PIN in the appropriate field and click Buy.

Note: to order and ship physical items, you must sign up for Google Checkout from a computer.

Why do i need a Checkout PIN number?

The Checkout PIN number presents a convenient way of verifying your account on a mobile device.

What should I enter for my Checkout PIN number?

Choose a secret number that is between 4 and 16 digits in length. You may use the numbers 0-9 to create your PIN.

Things to remember when choosing a secret Checkout PIN:

  • Enter a number only you know.
  • Don't use a number someone else can easily guess.
  • Don't use a number that you've used somewhere else.

How do I change my Checkout PIN number?

If you'd like to change your Checkout PIN number, follow these steps:

  1. Click Settings along the bottom of the Buy page.
  2. Enter your old PIN and your new PIN in the appropriate fields.
  3. Click Save.

I forgot my Checkout PIN number. What should I do?

If you only use Google Checkout from your mobile phone, you can create a new Checkout PIN by clicking Forgot PIN? on the Buy page.

Then, re-enter the credit card information you used to create your account.

Once you've entered your credit card details, you'll be prompted to select a new Checkout PIN.

If you also use Google Checkout from a desktop computer, you can create a new Checkout PIN by clicking Forgot PIN? on the Buy page.

Then, re-enter your login information.

Once you've entered your login information, you'll be prompted to select a new Checkout PIN.

Are my billing details stored on my mobile phone?

No. We store all of your account information on our secure servers.

We'll place a cookie on your phone to identify you when you return to make more purchases, but you can remove this cookie from your browser if you'd prefer.

Keep in mind that, if you delete your cookie, you'll be prompted to provide verification information each time you make a purchase.

Can I change my billing or shipping information from my mobile phone?

If you signed up for Google Checkout from a desktop computer, you can choose different payment methods or shipping addresses when making a purchase, as long as you've saved them in your Google Checkout account.

You'll need to sign in to your account from a computer to add or edit shipping or billing information.

If you've signed up for Google Checkout from a mobile device, you can upgrade your account to enter shipping information or edit billing information on the desktop.

Will I receive receipts for my mobile purchases?

If you already use Google Checkout on your computer, then you will continue to receive receipts for your mobile purchases.

You will also be able to view your mobile purchases on the Purchase History page of your account.

If you've only ever used Google Checkout from a mobile device and you'd like to receive email receipts for future Google Checkout mobile purchases, visit the Settings page to enter the email address at which you'd like to receive the receipts.

Here's how:

  1. Click Settings along the bottom of the Buy page.
  2. Enter your email address in the Change Email Address field.
  3. Click Save.

If you'd like to change the email address at which you receive receipts, simply visit the Settings page to enter the new email address.

You will also be able to view all your purchases on the Purchase History page of your account by visiting Google Checkout Home at checkout.google.com

I lost my mobile phone! What should I do?

Don't worry. If someone finds your phone, he or she would need to know your Checkout PIN number to access your Google Checkout account.

If the wrong PIN is entered more than 3 times, we'll invalidate the cookie on your phone and you will be prompted to sign in to your account again.

I have a new mobile phone. Can I still make purchases?

Yes, but you'll be prompted to provide verification information after you click Buy at a participating seller. Then, enter your existing Checkout PIN.

Can I access my Google Checkout account from multiple phones?

Yes. As long as the mobile phone you're using can connect to the Internet, you can sign in to Google Checkout.

Keep in mind that if you use multiple phones to make purchases, you'll be prompted to provide verification information the first time you use the phone to make a purchase.

You must use the same Checkout PIN you previously created.

Can I use Checkout on my mobile phone if I am roaming?

Yes. You can use Google Checkout as long as you can access the Internet.

There's a problem with my digital download. What should I do?

If there is a problem with your digital download, please contact the seller.

Make sure to include your 15-digit Google order number which can be found by viewing your order details at checkout.google.com

Is there a spend limit on mobile orders?

If you already use Google Checkout on your computer, there is no limit to how much you may purchase.

However, if you've only used Google Checkout from a mobile device, there is a limit of €50.00 per order.

Learn how to remove your spending limits.

top

Is there a limit to the total amount I can purchase with my Google Checkout account?

If you already use Google Checkout on your computer, there is no limit to how much you may purchase.

However, if you've only used Google Checkout from a mobile device, there is a limit of €1,000.00 per account.

Learn how to remove your spending limits.

top

How are account purchase limits calculated across currencies?

If you have a purchase limit on your account and you buy an item in a currency other than the currency of your purchase limit, we'll convert the price of the item into the currency of your purchase limit in order to measure the transaction against that limit.

Foreign currency rates are provided by Citibank N.A. and displayed under license.

Rates are for information purposes only and are subject to change without notice.

Who should I contact if I have a problem with Google Checkout on my mobile phone?

If you're having trouble using Google Checkout from your mobile phone, please visit the Google Checkout Help Center at http://google.com/cmh on your desktop computer.

To contact the Google Checkout Team directly, please visit http://google.com/cmc on your desktop computer.

Make sure to include your 15-digit Google order number which can be found by viewing your order details at checkout.google.com

If you're having trouble with an order you placed from your mobile phone, please contact the seller directly.
