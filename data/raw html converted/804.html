Suggest a feature for Maps

Thank you for taking an interest in the development of Maps. We appreciate hearing from you, and value all the help you offer.

Please select any features you'd like from the list below. While you won't receive a personal response, we'll review your feedback and keep it in mind as we work to improve Maps.

Map Views
Update Satellite imagery
Update Street View imagery
View maps in a specified language
Add trail information to Terrain view
Show county boundaries on the map

Searching
Search for businesses along a plotted route
Search within a specified radius

Route Information
Include walking and hiking trails in walking directions
Add the estimated cost of trips (gas, toll, etc.) for driving directions
Send driving directions to your phone via SMS

My Maps
Sort lines, placemarks, and shapes into folders
Easily email all collaborators
Change multiple placemaker icons simultaneously
Copy items from one map to another
