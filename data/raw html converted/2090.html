I love the concept. The gadget itself could use some improvements... I attempted to stream a station that uses its own flash player, and it didn't work, but the station itself is stuck on there....like a phantom. It doesn't show up as anything but a blank on the station list, and can't be erased. Also, I can't scroll on the station list past the first four stations. So if I want to delete the sixth or seventh station, for instance, I have to delete the fourth and fifth to get it up on the list where I can delete or work with it. But...as someone else said, it's free and it does work once you figure it out. For those who cant make it work, stream a station using windows media player, right click on the station name and select 'properties'. The url will appear at the bottom of the 'file' tab at 'location'. Copy this and paste it into 'station url' using 'ctrl' 'v'. Then press play in the middle. Delete the Best FM, as it doesnt work, and add as many as you want, being aware that once you add more than 4 you wont be able to access them to edit them later.
