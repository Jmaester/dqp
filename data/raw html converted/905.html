Pompeii is a town located about 150 miles south of Rome near Naples. In 79 AD, Mount Vesuvius erupted and buried the town, and thousands of its inhabitants, in volcanic ash and debris.

Pompeii was completely forgotten about until it was accidentally rediscovered in the 1500's.

Excavation of Pompeii began in the 1700's and continues to this day.
