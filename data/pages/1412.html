What can I do to make it more likely for my site's videos to get included in Google Video?

It's our goal to include every video on the Web in Google Video. To help make this goal a reality, we offer guidelines as well as tips for building a crawler-friendly site. While there's no guarantee that our spiders will find a particular site, following these guidelines should increase the chances of finding videos on your site in the Google Video search results.

Got a video site?
Reach more people by making your videos discoverable on Google. Make sure Google knows about your site's video content by submitting a Video Sitemap or a MRSS feed.
