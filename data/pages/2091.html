Description

Tata Motors Limited (Tata Motors) is an automobile company. The Company is engaged mainly in the business of automobile products consisting of all types of commercial and passenger vehicles, including financing of the vehicles sold by the Company. The Company has two segments: automotive and others. The others segment includes construction equipment, engineering solutions and software operations. Tata Motors has operations in the United Kingdom, South Korea, Thailand and Spain. On October 16, 2009, the Company has acquired 79% shares in Tata Hispano Motors Carrocera S.A. by way of exercise of the existing call option, through mutual agreement with the other share-holder, Investalia S.A., Spain. Consequently, Tata Hispano Motors Carrocera S.A. has become a 100% subsidiary of the Company. On March 30, 2010, the Company has sold 20% stake in Telco Construction Equipment Company Ltd (Telcon) to Hitachi Construction Machinery Co. Ltd.
