Digg Ads Sponsored news article placement appears inline with organic content. Use this performance-based product to amplify your message by using Digg as a launching pad for your story.

  • Terms & Conditions
  • Guidelines
  • Advertisers can create their own ad by entering a headline, URL, display URL, submitting a 1x1" thumbnail image and summary description that is viewable on the story item
  • Each ad has a "Sponsored by [Company name]" tag and appears as a story-item within the storylist
  • Advertiser competes in an auction which is a function of their cost-per-click bids and the number of Diggs
  • The ads must directly relate to the content on the landing page and must clearly represent the company, product, or brand that is being advertised.
