Partnership Opportunities

Digg works with a wide range of partners and our philosophy is that no partner is too big or too small. If you have ideas for how our companies can work together, we want to hear from you. You might also want to check out some of the programs we have in place to facilitate some of our most common partnership requests.

Digg This Buttons

Websites put Digg This buttons on their site to make it easier for their users to submit stories to Digg. When a story from your site gets enough votes to make it to the Digg homepage, you can expect to see a substantial spike in traffic. We have made integrating Digg This buttons very easy - just a matter of plugging some code into your page. You can see all the options and pick up the buttons on our Tools page. We recommend you install the Digg This button with the number count. Visit our Digg Button page for more information.

Digg API

Digg has a non-commercial API available that provides all the information you need to customize the Digg experience for your website. Some of our partners have used the API to make cool visual representations of what’s happening on Digg, or you could create a custom Digg experience that shows only the stories from your site that are being Dugg. Honestly, we don’t even know all the things you can do with the API, so we invite you to make some cool stuff that makes us look bad for not thinking of it first. You can pick up the API, and read the license terms for more details.

Digg Stories on your Website

We created a tool that lets you create a custom feed of Digg news to display on your own website. You could use the API to do this, but we’ve made it easier with the Digg Widget.

Digg Video Integration

You may have noticed that some of videos on Digg show a thumbnail, and others also have a play button that lets you view videos without leaving Digg. Many sites like YouTube have chosen to do this because if Diggers can watch the video on Digg, there is a better chance it will get voted to the homepage. We get a lot of requests for this and we are trying to accommodate as many as we can, but it is a custom process right now so please be patient. Follow these instructions to start the process.
