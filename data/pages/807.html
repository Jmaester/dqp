What can we help you with?

Our goal is to help you get the fastest answer to your question. Here's how you can find what you're looking for:

  • To change or remove information in Google's search results, you'll need to contact the webmaster of the site that contains the information in question. See more instructions
  • The fastest way to find answers is to look in our Help Center. It's full of answers to frequently asked questions, and we're continuously adding and updating information.
  • The next fastest way to get help is to post your question to the Help Forum. This community of users, together with a Google Guide who posts periodically, enjoys helping each other figure out the best ways to use our product.
  • Have a great idea or suggestion? Let us know! We enthusiastically read and tally all your suggestions. While we don't respond to these comments, we use them to help develop the product.
  • Need to contact our support team? While we do read each email, you'll only hear from us (generally within three business days) if we need more information to resolve your issue. Click a link to see information about troubleshooting or reporting your issue.
