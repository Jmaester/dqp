Google Video Known Issues

We're always working to resolve issues that pop up on Google Video. If your issue is listed below, we're aware of the issue and are working to fix it as soon as possible. To see more information on an issue, just click "View Details & Report." Clicking the "Report it" button will save you the time of sending us an email but will ensure that we're notified about your issue.

  • All my video reports are incorrect or showing zero.
    Since our team will be focusing on improving the search experience, the Insight feature has been discontinued.

    Having this issue?    

  • I deleted my video but it is still appearing in Google Video search results.
    Updating the search index is not instantaneous. Therefore, it may take some time for your video to disappear from the Google Video search results.

    Having this issue?    

  • I chose my preference to allow/restrict embedding & downloading of my video but it is not reflecting on the site.
    A transcoding issue resulted in some videos not correctly reflecting the preferences that were chosen.

    Having this issue?    

Don't see your issue listed above?

Look in our Help Center. It's full of answers to frequently asked questions, and we're continuously adding and updating information.
