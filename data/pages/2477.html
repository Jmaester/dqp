    Google Places

    Visit the Google Places homepage and click on the Country drop-down menu to see where this is available. In countries where listings can't be added using Google Places, you may be able to add these locations using Map Maker.

    Community Edits

    In some areas, you can edit business listing details using the Edit details link, which can be found in the drop-down menu from the information window.

    In other areas, you can move the marker of a location, but you can't edit any business details.
