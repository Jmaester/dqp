Contact us

You have reached the contact form for Google Checkout buyers. If you are a merchant and need assistance, please contact us here.

Google Checkout is happy to offer assistance whenever you need it. Follow the steps below to submit a question to our support team.

  1. Select the topic that most closely matches the theme of your question.
  2. Click Continue.
  3. Enter the required account and/or order information.
  4. Specify the relevant details related to your question.
  5. Click Submit to email your question to the support team.

We look forward to answering your question!
