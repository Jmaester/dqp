Gmail Known Issues

We're always working to resolve problems that come up in Gmail. If your problem is listed below, we're aware of the issue and are working to fix it as soon as possible. To see more information on an issue, just click View Details & Report. Clicking the 'Report it' button will save you the time of sending us an email, but it will ensure that we're notified about your issue.

Logging In

  • 'Oops 009' partially loads inbox in small rectangle
    Some users have experienced their mail loading in a small rectangle at the top, left corner of their screens. As a workaround, we suggest that Firefox users disable the Megaupload extension and that Internet Explorer users try signing in from Firefox, available for free download here, or from Google Chrome, available for free download here.

    Having this issue?    

  • Offline users - Unable to synchronize with http
    Gears treats http://mail.google.com and https://mail.google.com as separate URLs. If you originally synchronized your offline messages using http://mail.google.com, you won't be able to synchronize any offline content when you access Gmail through the HTTPS URL. You'll need to re-enable offline for Gmail on https://mail.google.com. Here's how.

    Having this issue?    

  • Inbox won't load. Directed to our 'basic HTML' view
    If you get stuck on 'Loading...,' we suggest first trying our troubleshooting steps. You should be able to use the 'older version' of Gmail or the basic HTML view while we work to resolve the issue.

    Having this issue?    

  • Notifier cannot connect to mailbox with 'Always use https' setting enabled
    *Note: If you've enabled the 'Always use https' setting in Google Mail, you'll need to install a patch for the Notifier to work with this setting:
    1. Download the patch (.zip).
    2. Open the folder.
    3. Double-click the notifier_https.reg file.
    4. Click yes when you're asked to confirm if you want to add the information to the registry.
    5. Restart the Notifier.

    If you decide you no longer want to use the https setting, you'll need to install the other file included in the download to reset the Notifier. Use the same method as above, except with 'notifier_https_undo.reg.'

    Having this issue?    

  • Sign in and redirect problems in UK, Canada, Australia
    Some users have had difficulties logging into their Gmail/Googlemail accounts, encountering strange redirects, multiple password prompts, the appearance of being signed in to another user's account, etc. This issue relates to malware on your computers or routers that is interfering with your access to certain sites on the internet. To fix, please run a virus scan and reset your router.

    Having this issue?    

Chat and Contacts

  • Last digit of phone numbers is cut off
    Firefox 3.6.8 users who have set a larger browser font size may not see full phone numbers in the Contact Manager. To resolve this problem, reduce your font size to 14. We're working to resolve this issue.

    Having this issue?    

  • Chats default 'off the record'
    Users may notice that chats are defaulting to 'off the record' even though 'Save chat history' is enabled. To temporarily resolve this, select 'Cancel' in the chat window, which will make the chat conversation go back 'on the record'. We are actively investigating this issue.

    Having this issue?    

  • 'You have been signed out of Gmail...'
    Some Firefox users have reported seeing the error, 'You have been signed out of Gmail. You must sign back in to reenable chat.' Please clear your cache to resolve the issue.

    Having this issue?    

  • Names and other text display as 'undefined'
    If you see names in your Chat list or messages displayed as 'undefined,' or if you see 'undefined' between the 'Save' and 'Discard' buttons when composing a message, please upgrade your Better Gmail Firefox extension to Better Gmail 2. If you have Greasemonkey enabled in your browser, please disable the 'Gmail Conversation Preview' script to resolve the issue.

    Having this issue?    

  • Updated picture shows up in Settings but old picture still shows in my Chat list
    If you've updated your picture in the 'General' tab of your Gmail settings, but when hovering over your name in your Chat list, you still see the old picture, check to make sure you haven't set a picture for yourself in your Contacts list. Click 'Contacts' on the left side of the page and search for your Gmail address. Select the entry for your address and check the picture on the right side of the page.

    Having this issue?    

  • Contacts export and print returning no response or error
    If you're trying to export your contacts and get no response, or trying to print contacts and get a 404 error, try refreshing Gmail in your browser. This should allow you to successfully export or print.

    Having this issue?    

  • "Loading" when editing contacts
    Some users are seeing a persistent "Loading..." message when trying to edit contacts. We're working to resolve this problem. In the meantime, you can use http://www.google.com/contacts to edit your contact information.

    Having this issue?    

  • Autocomplete not working in basic HTML view
    We are aware of an issue where contact autocomplete is not working for users in basic HTML view. While we investigate this, please use the standard view of Gmail to fix the problem.

    Having this issue?    

Composing and Reading Mail

  • Message background colors are displaying as white when 'Choose your own colors' theme is enabled
    If you're using the 'Choose your own colors' theme, colors may not be displayed correctly in certain fields.

    Having this issue?    

  • 'Archive,' 'Report Spam,' 'Delete,' 'Mark as read,' 'Move to,' and 'Labels' buttons are stacked on top of each other

    The buttons may appear vertically stacked if you're using Offline Gmail in Internet Explorer 8. This issue occurs when you are using 'Compatibility View' in IE8.

    To disable Compatibility View, open the 'Tools' menu and select 'Internet Options.' Click the Advanced tab, and in the 'Browsing' section, uncheck the box next to 'Automatically recover from page layout errors with Compatibility View.'

    Having this issue?    

  • Stuck on 'Loading...' when opening messages

    We're aware of an issue where users receive a 'Loading...' message when trying to open an email. We are currently investigating the issue.

    In the meantime, please try clearing your cache and cookies, using another browser, or using the basic HTML version of Gmail.

    Having this issue?    

  • Safari 3 - Unable to compose messages
    If clicking to compose a message doesn't seem to work, you can fix the problem by upgrading your browser. You can go to http://www.apple.com/safari/download/ to get the latest version of Safari.

    Having this issue?    

  • Freezing or slowness with HTML Validator installed
    Some users report that the Firefox extension HTML Validator causes slowness or crashing in their Gmail accounts. We suggest disabling this extension while we work to resolve the issue.

    Having this issue?    

  • Messages are improperly threaded
    At this time, you can't manually merge or separate messages from conversations.

    Having this issue?    

  • Thumbnails for attached images don't display
    Users report that thumbnails will stop displaying and be replaced by an icon. The problem often resolves itself and then reappears. In the meantime, clicking the 'View' or 'Download' links will enable you to see the images.

    Having this issue?    

  • Attachment downloads lead to a blank screen when Tab Mix Plus is installed
    In your Tab Mix Plus settings, select Prevent blank tabs when downloading files. If this doesn't resolve the issue, we suggest disabling the extension.

    Having this issue?    

  • Oops 103 errors
    For all non-Firefox 3 users: Please try logging in to your account through our secured interface, available via https://mail.google.com. Be sure to use this exact URL, with no characters after '.com.' If this resolves the issue, we suggest that you enable the 'Always use HTTPS' setting in your account. For Firefox 3 users: Please try accessing your mail from the older version of Gmail https://mail.google.com/mail/?ui=1&ov=1 while we work to resolve the issue. If this doesn't resolve the issue, please try another browser such as Google Chrome.

    Having this issue?    

  • Advanced attachment features can cause browser errors
    Users behind network proxies might encounter 'Attachment failed' and other errors when using the advanced attachment features, especially in Firefox 3. Change your attachment settings by selecting 'Basic attachment features' in the General tab of the Settings page.

    Having this issue?    

  • Stuck on 'Loading' when trying to compose mail, reply to mail, or access settings in Firefox
    Users who recently upgraded to Firefox 3.6.12 have been experiencing loading issues when trying to perform various actions in Gmail. We are currently investigating the problem.

    Having this issue?    

  • Seeing 'Oops #010' error when trying to use Gmail
    We're currently investigating the issue and have found this error is being seen on older versions of the Mozilla Firefox browser. We recommend updating to the latest version of Mozilla Firefox.

    Having this issue?    

POP & IMAP

  • IMAP clients timeout or crash when downloading very large folders
    IMAP clients aren't always designed to handle the huge amount of mail that users store in Gmail. If you have thousands of messages in your labels, we recommend doing some housework to clean up your labels in the web interface. Remember, the web interface shows counts of threads, not messages.

    Having this issue?    

  • 'Too many simultaneous connections'
    We've aware of an issue where users are receiving a 'too many simultaneous connections' message when using IMAP. We are currently looking into this issue.

    In the meantime, please ensure you've closed/signed out of any mail clients you are not actively using.

    Having this issue?    

  • Folder subscriptions don't work in Apple Mail 3

    Folder subscriptions in Apple Mail 3 are not compatible with Gmail IMAP access at this time (folders don't populate in the 'Subscription List').

    In the meantime, you may want to try a different mail client, such as Mozilla Thunderbird.

    Having this issue?    

  • Non-Latin characters can corrupt message headers

    Message headers contain technical information necessary for the successful delivery of messages between email servers. Gmail's IMAP implementation re-encodes the information stored in message headers, but non-ASCII characters may become garbled. For example, this can affect the 'To:' line in an email message if a name is written in a language that uses non-Latin characters. Several issues can result from corrupt message headers, including delivery problems.

    The Gmail Team is working to resolve this issue.

    Having this issue?    

Google Buzz

  • Tweets not showing up in Buzz in real time

    Buzz crawls Twitter on a regular basis and we are still tuning that cycle length. This can result in your tweets showing up in batches in Buzz rather than in real time.

    Having this issue?    

  • Unable to comment on Buzz pulled in from Reader
    Google Reader sharing settings control who can comment on buzz posts that originate as Google Reader shared items. If you aren't included in the poster's Google Reader sharing groups, you won't be able to comment.

    Having this issue?    

  • "Load more" doesn't always show or respond
    Some Google Buzz users are having trouble with the "Load more" link at the bottom of the page. In some cases, it doesn't appear, and in others, it doesn't respond. You may be able to resolve the problem by clearing your browser's cache and cookies.

    Having this issue?
