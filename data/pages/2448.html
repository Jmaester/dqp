Does Google Desktop search email attachments?

Comment Print
If your email has been indexed by Google Desktop, Desktop should also be able to index the file names of your attachments. If the attachment is a text file, Google Desktop should also index its content. For best results, you may want to download the attachment to a searchable location on your computer.
