Search across languages

Google Translated Search

Sometimes the best results for your search query won't be in your language. To make your search truly global, try our "Translated Search" option in Google Search. It determines which languages have the best information for your query and translates web results from those languages back to yours.

Try it now:

"Translate this page" links

If you search for a foreign term on Google Search, you may encounter search results not in your native language. Simply click the "Translate this page" link by the search result to view a machine translation of the page on Google Translate.

Try it now: Search on Google for Deutscher Bundestag.

Translate directly from Google Search

For simple translations, you don't even have to leave the Google search results page. Just type your translation query into the search box and see the translation result appear immediately.

Try it now: Search on Google for Translate Apfel or Translate 'My Name is Jeff' into Spanish.

Explore the multilingual web

Google Chrome

Try Google Chrome, our fast, secure and multilingual browser. Similar to Google Toolbar, Chrome automatically detects when you visit a page in a foreign language and translates the text into your native language instantly.

Try it now: Download Google Chrome.

Google Toolbar

Google Toolbar is a browser add-on for Internet Explorer and Firefox that automatically detects web pages that are written in foreign languages and translates the text of those pages into your native language.



Try it now: Download Google Toolbar. (Requires Microsoft Internet Explorer or Mozilla Firefox)

Communicate globally

Gmail

What if you receive an important email – but it's not in your language? If you use Gmail, it can automatically translate foreign email messages into your own language. Just enable the feature in Gmail Labs. Here’s how.

While in Gmail, you can also have a multilingual chat with your pen-pal from overseas. Add the Google Translate Chat Bot to your conversation and break down the language barrier.

Google Groups

Ever wanted to have a discussion with people from across the world? Take a look at the translate features in Google Groups – you can post messages in your language and see responses from users from all over the world, translated into your language. We love the feature and use it on our own discussion group to connect with our users.

Try it now: Visit the Google Translate Discussion Group.

Professional, business and developer tools

Machine translation is an excellent way to expose your website or business to a global audience. Here are some of the ways that Google Translate can help you and your business:

Google Translate AJAX API

Engineer a translation solution for your website, using the Google Translate AJAX API. Learn more on our Google Code page.

Translator Toolkit

Are you a professional translator? You can increase your productivity by using Google Translator Toolkit. Translator Toolkit features WYSIWYG UI for context when translating, machine translation, glossaries and the ability to upload and download your translation memories.
