Known Issues Page

You can use the list below to find out if your problem is a known issue. If the problem is listed here, we're aware of it and are working to fix it as soon as possible.

If you're experiencing issues with your Google Places listing, check out the Google Places Known Issues page.

If you're experiencing issues with Map Maker, check out the Google Map Maker Issue Tracker page.

Please note: Due to high volume, we're unable to answer email about our known issues. When you click the 'Let us know' button, you'll send a report and help us to determine how many people are experiencing the issue.

Commonly reported issues in Google Maps

  • Some parts of the map don't finish loading

    Tiles may not be available due to user latency issue. Try refreshing your browser.

    Having this issue?    

  • I've noticed an error in the base map data for Google Maps

    If the error occurs in map data for Australia, Austria, Belgium, Canada, Denmark, Lichtenstein, Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, South Africa, Switzerland, or the United States, please use the Report A Problem function.

    If the error occurs outside of the aforementioned countries, please visit our Fix an error page for more options.

    Having this issue?    

  • Draggable Driving directions doesn't work.

    Please try again in another browser or ensure your current browser is up to date.

    Having this issue?    

Commonly reported issues in Google Street View

  • My house on Street View isn't labeled correctly

  • I have found imagery that I would like to be removed

    You can request removal of the imagery by clicking 'Report a problem' in the bottom left corner of the imagery. You can also read how to Report a Problem.
