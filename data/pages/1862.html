Overview

You can edit your payment or billing address information at any time. If you don't want to save your payment information in your Google Account, you can delete it after making a purchase. But remember, you must have at least one payment method in your account in order to make purchases or receive refunds.

Update a Card

  1. Sign in to your account at https://checkout.google.com/.
  2. Click Edit payment methods along the left side of the page.
  3. Click Edit under the card information you would like to change. Once you've made the desired updates, click Save.
    • If you'd like to use this card as your default payment method, click Make this my default payment method.
  4. Click Save.

Add a Card

  1. Sign in to your account at https://checkout.google.com/.
  2. Click Edit payment methods along the left side of the page.
  3. Enter your credit/debit card details and billing address under Add a new payment method.
    • If you'd like to use this card as your default payment method, click Make this my default payment method.
  4. Click Save.

Delete a Card

  1. Sign in to your account at https://checkout.google.com/.
  2. From the Purchase History page, click Edit payment methods, depending on what you'd like to delete.
  3. Click Delete next to the address or card information that you'd like to delete.
