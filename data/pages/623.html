Mobile

Digg’s mobile apps are currently unavailable due to known issues with our API. Once these issues are resolved, we will release the apps again. In the meantime, you can access m.digg.com via your mobile device. Thanks for your patience.

Take Digg with you wherever you go with a range of mobile offerings. Each is optimized for different mobile devices so you have a few options:

iPhone app

Digg’s official app for the iPhone provides a seamless experience for browsing popular content from around the web and engaging in the conversations around that content.

Android app

Digg’s official Android app is a great mobile browsing experience designed with the platform in mind. Browse stories and comments while being able to participate in the social news curation experience.

Digg mobile (m.digg.com)

Digg's mobile site is optimized to run on pretty much any mobile browser. Login, browse and Digg your favorite stories and view a sample of the top comments for each story.
