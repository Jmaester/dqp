Description

Magyar Telekom Telecommunications Public Limited Company (Magyar Telekom) is a provider of fixed line telecommunications services in Hungary. It operates under the name Magyar Telekom Plc. Magyar Telekom is the principal supplier of telecommunications services in Hungary, Macedonia and Montenegro and alternative service provider in Bulgaria, Romania and in the Ukraine. The Group's operating segments in Hungary are: Consumer Services Business Unit, Business Services Business Unit, Group Headquarters and Technology Business Unit.
