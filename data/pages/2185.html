How do I report copyright infringement?

Comment Print

Google is committed to protecting the rights of copyright holders. If you believe a video provider is offering content on Google Video without proper rights or authorization, you should always try to contact the video provider directly, where possible. (For example, in many cases, a contact URL will appear on the video's description page.)

If you're a copyright owner or an authorized representative of the copyright owner, you can notify us of an alleged copyright infringement in accordance with the process outlined in our Copyright Policy.

To expedite our ability to process your request, complaints may now also be submitted online. Please continue to provide us with the details of your complaint.

Based on the video's playback page URL, where is the video hosted?

The video's playback page is an external, third-party website. Although the video appears in Google Video search results, it is hosted by another site.

Because it is located on a publicly available third-party webpage, removal from our index doesn't mean it's no longer available on the Web. For this reason, we encourage you to contact the webmaster of the site in question. Once the webmaster makes these changes, Google's search results will update automatically when it next crawls the page.

Would you like to file a copyright complaint and request removal of a video from our search index?

Return to Google Video

In order to expedite the processing of your claim, please provide the following information regarding the video:

  • The title of the video in question
  • The URL of the Google Video search results page where you located the video
  • The playback page URL of the video (located at the hosting site)

Please continue to provide us with the details of your complaint.

Since we previously offered an uploading feature on Google Video, you may come across some videos that are hosted by Google Video. For these videos, the playback page URL has the following format: http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid= , followed by a series of numbers (like this: http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=3383042311441257769).

Please continue to provide us with the details of your complaint.

For information on contacting YouTube, please visit http://www.youtube.com/t/contact_us
