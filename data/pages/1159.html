Privacy Complaint Guidelines, Part 2

Comment Print

Make sure that if you are filling out the privacy complaint that you are the individual or that you are the legal guardian of the individual shown in the video. We will not accept forms filled out for your co-worker, spouse, sibling, or friend. Please note we will not take action unless you are clearly identifiable in the video.

We will not accept third party privacy complaints for video removals.
