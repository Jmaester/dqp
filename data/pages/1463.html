Breaking: Breaking News

<< Back to Blog
Submitted by Can Duruk on November 11, 2010 - 10:26am
  • Community

Digg is an amazing place to find out what's happening in the world. From the latest videos to trapped miners to midterm elections, it's awesome how news makes its way to Digg.

As part of our effort to surface more great content on Digg, we came up with a feature that allows us to both showcase breaking/interesting stories quickly as well as aggregate stories on a particular topic. Our community team will be managing the content in this module and working hard to identify the best stories out there on Digg. You can see the module on the right side of the Top News, My News and Upcoming pages.

I hope you get as much enjoyment out of using the module as I did working on it - thanks also to Will, Jamie, Tyson, and Mike for helping get this out.

Thanks everyone, and happy digging!

Can D.
