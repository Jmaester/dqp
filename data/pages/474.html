Breaking: Illinois Legalized Same-Sex Civil Unions

blogs.riverfronttimes.com — Today in Illinois, both houses of the state legislature approved a bill granting civil unions to same-sex couples. The bill says that a civil union, expressly stated as between "two persons of either the same or opposite sex," entitles the parties of the union to the "same legal obligations, responsibilities, protections and benefits afforded or recognized by the law of Illinois to spouses." It also says that Illinois recognizes civil unions or similar unions entered into in other jurisdictions, and provides a mechanism for them to be dissolved. 3 hr 13 min ago
