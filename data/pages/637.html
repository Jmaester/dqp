Ways to correct business information

Sometimes you may see incorrect information with particular businesses on Google Maps; for example, an individual location may have recently closed or a phone number is incorrect. One of the exciting things about maps and geography is that the world is a constantly changing place, and keeping on top of these changes is a never-ending endeavor. It's the sort of challenge we like to take on here at Google, and we want to give you the opportunity to help. After all, the best experts on a place are the people that live there. Read below to learn how you can help make Google Maps a better place.

What can I edit?
Making corrections to business listings is easy! Here’s what you can edit directly on Maps:
  • Place name
  • Phone number
  • Homepage
  • Category
  • Address
  • Marker location

For all other issues you might see (for example: spam, place has another listing, a place is closed, or listings that violate our Business Listing Quality Guidelines), use the Report a problem link to tell us about it.


Where can I find the Edit link?
  • Click more from the info window and select Edit details
  • Click Edit this place at the top of the business' Place Page
  • Click Edit this place at the bottom of a business' Place Page
  • Click the triangle next to a business listing on the search results on Maps and select Edit details

How do I make an edit?
  1. Click the Edit link.
  2. Make the appropriate changes.
  3. Click the Publish button, and you're finished.

What can I report a problem on?
If you can’t edit a piece of information directly on Maps, you’ll need to report a problem to get it corrected. Here’s a list of reports you can tell us about:
  • Listing contains incorrect information or spam
  • Place is permanently closed
  • Place has another listing
  • Place doesn’t exist or is private
  • Some photos, reviews, or details belong to a different place

Where can I find the Report a problem link?
  • Click more from the info window and select Report a problem
  • Click more on the business' Place Page and select Report a problem
  • Click Report a problem at the bottom of a business' Place Page
  • Click the triangle next to a business listing on the search results on Maps and select Report a problem
  • Click Report a problem below the search results on Maps
  • Click Report a problem at the bottom right of the map
  • Right click on the map and select Report a problem
  • Click Report a problem below driving directions

How do I report a problem?
  1. Click the Report a problem link.
  2. Let us know what is wrong with the listing.
  3. Leave a comment to give us more specific information.
  4. Click the Report problem button, and you're finished.

Why can't I make an edit or report a problem?

If the Edit or Report a problem link is greyed out or doesn’t exist, this means that you’re not able to tell us about these issues just yet. Don’t worry, we’re working hard to make this available in all countries! To see which edits you can make in your country, visit this list.

Until then, contact your local data provider to edit and remove information from your business listing and don’t forget to report your issue on our Known Issues page.

To see how you can correct specific parts of a business listing, check out this article.
