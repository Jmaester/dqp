Description

IPG Photonics Corporation (IPG) designs and manufactures a line of fiber lasers and fiber amplifiers for applications in a range of markets, such as materials processing, applications, communications and medical. The Company sells its products worldwide to original equipment manufacturers (OEMs), system integrators and end users. It markets its products internationally primarily through its direct sales force, and also through agreements with independent sales representatives and distributors. IPG designs and manufactures components used in its finished products, including semiconductor diodes, optical fiber preforms and cables, finished fiber lasers and amplifiers. It also makes packaged diodes, direct diode laser systems and communications systems that utilize its optical fiber-based products. In January 2010, it acquired Photonics Innovations, Inc. In April 2010, the Company acquired Cosytronic KG (COSY).
