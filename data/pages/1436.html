Other Legal Issues

To report a violation of our policies, please use the "Report Problem" tab below the video and mark the video with one of the following categories:
  • Pornography or obscenity
  • Graphic violence
  • Racially or ethnically hateful content
  • Other content inappropriate for young viewers

Allegations of Defamation
  • We do not remove video postings due to allegations of defamation because we are not in a position to adjudicate the veracity of those postings. Consistent with Section 230(c) of the Communications Decency Act, we recommend that people pursue any legal claims they may have directly against the person who posted the video. If those claims result in a court order that requires us to remove the video from our service, we will respond accordingly. You may forward this court order to: Google, Inc. Attn: Google Video, Legal Complaints, 1600 Amphitheater Parkway Mountain View, CA 94043 or via fax to (650) 963-3299.
